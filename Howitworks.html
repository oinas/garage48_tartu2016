How Cico works

Have you ever missed a product from your home country or any other country?
You are in need of the product you miss, yet you cannot find it in the country you are living in!! Cico can solve this problem--we can make it possible for you by connecting travelers and requesters

Here's how it works?

  • You Sign Up For CICO
  • Complete Your Profile
  • Look For Traveler/Requester according to country based search
  • Request Traveler Your Desired Product From The Country
  • If Traveler Accepts Your Request. You Can Talk And Request Any Product You Like

Create A Profile

First step? Completely fill out your Cico profile! This will help you achieve trustworthy status.

Don't have an account yet? Sign Up

Search For Traveler Or Requester

Search according to country in the search bar! You can find profiles according to countries. You can contact them by clicking the request button. A request box with the functionality of attaching image will be provided. Attach the image of the product, click submit, wait for reply from traveler.
